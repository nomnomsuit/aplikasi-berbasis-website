About Us

Vintape.lust was born in 2022 at the east side of Jakarta, Indonesia. From our college project, we decided to start this journey. We're born out to spread the things that we love and cherish out into the world. Stating the obvious that we are all geeks, lovers, and collectors of music merchandise, vinyls, CDs, cassettes, shirts, you name it. Vintape.lust is a creative back-yard to express our ideas for music videos, graphic designs, illustra- tions, and all there is to visual arts.
